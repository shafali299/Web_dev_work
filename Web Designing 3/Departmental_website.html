COMPUTER SCIENCE & INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Overview

The horizons of Computer Science and Information Technology envelopes numerous aspects of computing and ICT. The integration of Information Technology and Computer Science has led to tremendous increase in the growth of computing and technology and widened the spectrum of opportunities. This department equips the students to become professionals who can leverage the expertise of both CSE & IT and be at the forefront of learning emerging technologies that can be applied in building computing and ICT systems that benefit the society.

The department aims to deliver high quality outcome-based education to students and imparts practical exposure through problem solving, project-based learning and streamlines students on latest tools and technologies.
